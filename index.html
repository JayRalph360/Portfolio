AI Travels, Self Driving Cars

One such field is transport, where AI applications are already disrupting the way we move people and goods. From scanning traffic patterns to reduce road accidents and optimizing sailing routes to minimize emissions, AI is creating opportunities to make transport safer, more reliable, more efficient and cleaner.

Using object detection models, opencv, python and machine learning technology, I have been able to build an AI that can detect parking spaces in a parking lot and send messages to subscribers in real time notifying them about these spaces, and if a space gets taken by another vehicle after a subscriber got notified, it would send another notification telling the subscriber the space is no more available. This has been one of the most demanding projects I have ever worked on
